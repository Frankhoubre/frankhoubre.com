# NEWS_SOURCES.md — Where the news loop looks

Niche: AI for cinematic creation — generative video, image, audio/music, and
the production workflow around them. Audience: creators, founders, freelancers
making ads, shorts, series and films with AI.

The news loop searches these, prioritizes the last 24-72h, falls back to the
last 7 days, prefers official/primary sources, and uses 2+ sources per article.

## Sources to avoid

- Anonymous content farms and SEO blogs with no byline.
- Pure rumor/leak aggregators stated as fact (mark clearly as rumor if used).
- Reposts that just rewrite an official announcement with no added reporting.
- Paywalled articles (do not bypass; find the primary source instead).
- Anything that cannot be corroborated by a second source within the window.

## Rules of engagement

- Never publish a rumor as a fact. Separate fact / analysis / opinion.
- Quote sparingly, link generously, never copy paragraphs.
- Every news article answers: what happened, why it matters, who is affected,
  what a creator should learn, how it connects to cinematic AI, the practical
  takeaway.
- Log every source URL used in CONTENT_LEDGER.md for the article.
